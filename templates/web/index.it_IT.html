I like to have the controll of the both side PHP and JS, but even have good and clean html and css/less enviroments. I try to do use some cool MVC framework like Simphony or CakePHP. But that was simply to much for build simpled and custom app, and most of everything I hate loose the controll of the query, there is the best part for optimize you website or webapp for running fast. I don't fit well in the MVC style, I take the concept of Module, wich I rappresent in libs/ and in the template requirement in /modules (this i for define a block).
